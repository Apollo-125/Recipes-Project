Spaghetti Bolognese Recipe

This is a delicious, simple dish created from a combination of Jamie Oliver and Marco Pierre-White's methods. enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Beef mince - 500g
  • Onion - 1 large
  • Carrots - 2 medium, diced
  • Pancetta ham (Unsmoked) - 50g (One packet)
  • Chopped tomato - 1 can
  • Tomato puree - 2 tbls
  • Bay leaves - 3 medium
  • Beef/Vegetable stock - 500ml
  • Red wine - (Optional)
  • Spaghetti
  • Basil
  • Oregano
  • Garlic - 3 to 4 cloves
  • Olive oil, salt & pepper

Method

  1. Heat 2 tbls of olive oil in a skillet or large frying pan on medium to high heat
  2. Add the onion and garlic and cook until fragrant or the onion is translucent
  3. Add the carrot and cook for a further 3-5 minutes or until it has started to soften
  4. Separate the mixture to create space in the pan and add the pancetta ham, cook until browned
  5. Add the beef mince and cook for 3-5 minutes or until browned and reduced in size
  6. Add a splash red wine to de-glaze the pan,
  7. Add the chopped tomatoes, tomato puree and the stock to the mixture and stir
  8. Add the bay leaves, basil and oregano to taste, along with salt & pepper
  9. Cook for around 30-40 minutes, meanwhile cook spaghetti according to packet instructions
  10. Serve and enjoy!
